Recommendations

The following is the recommendation for your trust, resilience and diversity trait based on your score. To get more recommendations, here is the link.

You are high in Trust.

Since you are confident in others intentions and commitments, you can actively speak out about challenges. You acknowledge other's skills and abilities, encourage them to reach their potential, all while supporting their learning. To further elevate this, seek people’s advice to demonstrate your trust in others actively.

You are high in Resilience. 

Since you have a supportive environment around this allows you to take on a positive mindset that makes your ambitions possible. To continue to improve this, take care of yourself - pay attention to your own needs and feelings. Engage in activities that you enjoy and find relaxing. Exercise regularly. Taking care of yourself helps to keep your mind and body primes to deal with situations that require resilience.

You are average in Diversity.

To continue to improve, 'Wait and See' -- Don't jump to conclusions! Digest perspectives that are different to yours by reflecting upon the new information that was presented to you.
